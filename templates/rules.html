Rules !


Now that you are a part of our community, you must know of some rules we follow around here.


  • Make sure you register for the event before starting. Otherwise, you are not allowed to participate in the event.
  • Make sure to verify the e-mail prior to the event. This ensures your eligibility for the event.
  • Make sure you remember your password. Without the password, you won't be able to log-in into the event and won't be able to participate.
  • When you start the CTF's, 24 hours will be alloted to complete the whole challenge.
  • Each question has equal points and are to be solved in a particular order.
  • Flags found are of the format Flag{some_text} . Some of the files may contain just the inner text within the brackets.
  • Make sure you submit it in the format specified. Flag text is not case sensitive.
  • Internet access is granted. Feel free to explore and read about the concept behind the problem.
  • This is a competitive environment with the aim of learning cyber security and ethical hacking. Please do not share the flags and solutions with others.
  • Ranks can be viewed on the Hackerboard page. The Hackerboard time is updated evertime a submission is made.
  • For hints, you have to keep your eyes and ears open.
  • Read between the lines to capture a particular flag.
